FAQs

How do I use my PIN?
Enter your 4-digit PIN whenever prompted to unlock actions.
How can I view my passwords?
Tap “See Passwords” and enter your PIN to reveal the list.
How do I change my Master Password?
Open Settings (🔑), select “Change Master Password,” and follow the prompts.
What if I forget my PIN?
Use your Master Password in the PIN prompt to reset it from Settings.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. On first launch, set your Master Password and 4-digit PIN.
2. Tap “Add Password” and fill in the fields.
3. Use “See Passwords” to view, copy, or delete entries.
4. Export your data via “Export” to back up.
5. Import a backup JSON file via “Import.”

Troubleshooting

I can’t open a modal window.
Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser, then refresh the page.
My password won’t save.
Make sure you have set a PIN and entered it correctly when saving.
I forgot my PIN or Master Password.
Use the “Reset App” button and enter your Master Password to clear all data and start fresh.
Import failed with an error.
Check that you selected a valid JSON backup and entered the correct Master Password or PIN.

Security Tips

  • Use a unique, strong Master Password you don’t use anywhere else.
  • Change your PIN periodically, especially after any suspected breach.
  • Back up your data before making major changes.
  • Keep your browser and device updated with the latest security patches.
  • Log out or clear local storage if you share your device with others.
